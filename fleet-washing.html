Common Questions

Fleet Washing FAQ

Do you need access to our water supply?

No. Our mobile wash trailer is self-contained with its own water supply, cleaning chemicals, and wastewater recovery system. We do not need to connect to your plumbing or drains.

How long does it take to wash a full fleet?

It depends on the number and size of vehicles. A single semi truck and trailer takes roughly 20 to 30 minutes. A fleet of 10 to 15 vehicles can usually be completed in a half day. We will give you a time estimate when we scope the job so you can plan accordingly.

Can you wash vehicles on a recurring schedule?

Absolutely. Many of our fleet clients set up weekly, biweekly, or monthly washing schedules. Recurring service comes with volume pricing and priority scheduling. We will work with your dispatch team to find a cadence that keeps your fleet clean year-round.

What areas do you serve for fleet washing?

We serve commercial fleets across the Omaha metro area including Bellevue, Papillion, La Vista, Gretna, Elkhorn, Council Bluffs, and surrounding areas in Nebraska and western Iowa. If your yard is within driving distance, we can make it work.

What about wastewater and environmental regulations?

We take environmental compliance seriously. Our wash trailer includes a wastewater recovery system that captures dirty water and prevents it from entering storm drains. We handle collection and disposal following local requirements so you do not have to manage it.
